STAY

Surrender to the pleasures of heavenly indulgence and unrivaled privacy. With lush greens, swaying palms and serene pool views, every villa has its own private garden in the front with huge windows as if extending into the landscape. Nestled cosily, our villas play hide & seek. Open and airy, yet warm and inviting, the interiors feel like home away from home. Sit under nature's parasol, stretch on deck chairs and explore open spaces which are refreshingly breezy. Very modern. Very in.



Premium Rooms

A natural extension to the serenity of nature, these newly made Studio rooms are designed to exude a vibe of contemporary living. The rooms open up to a balcony with a view of greenery in all its glory. High-end fittings and fixtures along soft colours create for a truly relaxing environment.

Size : 380 Sq.Ft.



Green Wood Villa

Feel at home in the warm and inviting Green Wood Villa. Equipped with a host of features and amenities, the villa features a sitting Area, spacious bedroom and a bathroom with a cubicle. The villa infuses you with a sense of relaxation and calm.

Size : 380 Sq.Ft.



Nizam Suite

Get swaddled by comfort and serenity in our stylish Nizam Suite. Explore our luxurious living room, 2 magnificent bedrooms with attached bathroom and enjoy your moments of peace. Spend moments in a small private sit out, get a view of our iconic penthouse (Jewel of Nizam - The Minar) and let time just fly by.

Size: 850 Sq. Ft.



Duplex Pool Villa

This double-storied villa makes your stay truly comfortable and memorable, with 2 bedrooms and a private garden. The Duplex Villa is the perfect choice for a family vacation.

Size : 1180 Sq.Ft.
